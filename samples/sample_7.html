FAQ

Questions, answered

How long does setup take?
Most teams are fully set up in under 30 minutes. Our onboarding wizard walks you through connecting your CRM, email, and calendar step by step. We also offer a free white-glove onboarding call for Pro and Enterprise plans.
Does [SaaSName] replace my existing CRM?
No — we're the AI layer on top of your existing stack. [SaaSName] syncs bidirectionally with Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, and 200+ other tools. Your reps keep using what they know; we just make it smarter.
How does the AI learn my sales style?
The AI analyzes your historical email conversations, call transcripts, and deal outcomes to build a model of what works for your specific product and ICP. Pro plans can also manually train on custom scripts and playbooks.
Is my data secure?
Yes. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and your data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We never sell your data or use it to train models for other customers. Enterprise plans support on-premise deployment.
Can I cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Month-to-month plans can be cancelled anytime from your dashboard with no fees. Annual plans can be cancelled for a full refund within 30 days; after that, access continues until the period ends.
Do you offer a free trial?
Yes — all plans include a 14-day free trial with full feature access and no credit card required. If you need more time to evaluate, just email our team and we'll extend it.
