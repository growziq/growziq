Our Story

MADE FROM SCRATCH,EVERY DAY

"My grandmother's recipe book is still the most valuable thing in our kitchen." Priya Nair, Founder

Sweet Treats began in a small home kitchen in 2012. What started as weekend batches for family gatherings grew into a beloved local institution, built on one simple belief: real ingredients, real craft, and real care.

Every confection is made fresh daily. No preservatives, no shortcuts. Just seasonal fruits, locally sourced dairy, single-origin chocolate, and recipes that have been passed down for generations.
