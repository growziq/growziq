1 Who We Are

Growziq is a web design and development business operating at growziq.in. We provide services to businesses in India. For data-related matters, contact us at dev.growziq@gmail.com.

Growziq is a Data Fiduciary under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act) to the extent applicable to our operations. We process personal data only to deliver the services you have contracted with us.

2 Data We Collect and Why

We collect only the minimum data necessary to deliver our services:

Data Collected Purpose and Legal Basis
Full name, business name, business type (intake form) Identify you, create project record, communicate. Legal basis: performance of contract + consent (DPDP Act).
Email address (intake form) Send scope email, invoice, updates, handover. Legal basis: performance of contract.
WhatsApp / phone number (intake form) Project communication, confirmations, support. Legal basis: performance of contract.
Business description (intake form) Understand project requirements. Legal basis: performance of contract.
Selected plan and add-ons (intake form) Generate correct scope, invoice, deliverables. Legal basis: performance of contract.
Consent record - timestamp, IP, form version Evidence of informed consent. Legal basis: legal obligation (DPDP Act).
Payment transaction ID, amount, date (Razorpay) Financial record, audit trail. Legal basis: legal obligation (Income Tax Act - 7-year retention).
Client-provided content (logos, photos, text, links) Build website per confirmed scope. Legal basis: performance of contract.
Hosting / domain credentials (temporary) Deploy your website. Legal basis: performance of contract. Deleted immediately on handover.
Project communications - email and WhatsApp Document project history, support dispute resolution. Legal basis: legitimate interest.

We do NOT collect: Aadhaar, PAN, bank account details, card numbers, biometric data, health data, or any sensitive personal data. Payment card data is processed entirely by Razorpay - we never see or store it.

3 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Delivering the web design and development service you contracted - building, deploying, and handing over your website.
  • Communicating throughout the project - scope emails, invoices, updates, handover, support.
  • Generating invoices and maintaining financial records as required by Indian tax law.
  • Responding to support requests during the 7-day Support Period.
  • Maintaining records for dispute resolution.
  • Complying with legal obligations including responding to lawful government requests.

We do NOT use your data for: advertising, marketing to third parties, profiling, selling to third parties, or any purpose unrelated to your project.

4 Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the stated purpose or as required by law:

Data Type Retention Period and Reason
Name, business name, email, phone (intake form) 30 days after project completion (CONFIRM date). DPDP Act - deleted after purpose is fulfilled.
Inactive / closed project data (no CONFIRM reached) 30 days after Day 45 project closure (Day 75 from payment). DPDP Act - deleted after project closure.
Consent record - timestamp, IP, form version 36 months. DPDP Act - consent record-keeping obligation.
Razorpay invoices and payment records 7 years from transaction date. Income Tax Act, 1961 - mandatory financial retention.
Scope Confirmation Email and SCOPE CONFIRMED reply 36 months. Limitation Act, 1963 - 3-year contract dispute window.
Handover email and delivery records 36 months. Limitation Act, 1963 - dispute evidence.
All project emails and WhatsApp communications 24 months. IT Act, 2000 - electronic evidence preservation.
Client-provided content - logos, images, text, assets Deleted within 30 days of CONFIRM. Purpose fulfilled.
Design files and staging environments Deleted within 30 days of CONFIRM. Purpose fulfilled.
Hosting and domain credentials Deleted immediately upon Handover. Access purpose fulfilled.

WhatsApp: We archive communications for the periods above. However, WhatsApp/Meta may retain messages on their servers per their own policy - outside our control. Our deletion is 'where technically feasible.'

Financial records exception: Invoices and payment records cannot be deleted even upon your erasure request - legally mandated for 7 years. We will notify you in writing of what is retained and the legal basis.

6 Your Rights Under the DPDP Act, 2023

As a Data Principal, you have the following rights:

Your Right How to Exercise It
Access Request a summary of what data Growziq holds about you. Email dev.growziq@gmail.com - we respond within 7 business days.
Correction Request correction of inaccurate data. Implemented within 7 business days of identity verification.
Erasure Request deletion of your data. Project data deleted within 30 days where technically feasible. Legally required financial records cannot be deleted - we will explain in writing what is retained and why.
Grievance Redressal File a complaint at dev.growziq@gmail.com. Acknowledged within 2 business days, resolved within 7.
Nominate Nominate someone to exercise your rights in the event of your death or incapacity.
Withdraw Consent Withdraw consent at any time by emailing dev.growziq@gmail.com. May affect service delivery. Does not affect legally retained records.

To exercise any right: email dev.growziq@gmail.com with subject 'Data Rights Request - [Your Name].' We verify your identity before acting on any request.

7 Consent

  • Consent is obtained at intake form submission via a mandatory, opt-in checkbox.
  • The checkbox records your agreement to this Privacy Policy, the Terms of Service, and your confirmation of age and authority.
  • Consent metadata recorded: timestamp, IP address, form version. Retained for 36 months.
  • Consent is freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous under the DPDP Act 2023. The checkbox is not pre-ticked.
  • You may withdraw consent at any time by emailing dev.growziq@gmail.com. Withdrawal does not apply retroactively to processing already performed.

8 Data Security

  • Growziq implements reasonable measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or disclosure.
  • Specific measures: project data restricted to Growziq partners only; credentials deleted on handover; communications archived on password-protected accounts; payment processing via Razorpay only - we never store card data.
  • In the event of a personal data breach likely to cause harm, we will notify you at your registered email as soon as practicable, in compliance with DPDP Act obligations.
  • No storage or transmission method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9 Cookies

  • growziq.in uses only essential cookies required for basic website functionality.
  • No advertising cookies or behavioral profiling without your explicit consent.
  • Google Analytics may be present for basic traffic analysis with a consent mechanism in place. Data is anonymous and aggregated.
  • The Tally intake form may set functional cookies governed by Tally's cookie policy.

10 Children's Data

Our services are for persons above 18 or business entities. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals below 18.

If we become aware of such data being inadvertently collected, we will delete it immediately.

11 Cross-Border Transfers

Some of our processors (Tally, Zapier, Google, Meta/WhatsApp) may store data on servers outside India. By consenting to this Policy, you acknowledge and consent to such transfers as they are incidental to delivering your project.

Growziq operates from India and does not intentionally transfer your data outside India beyond processor usage.

12 Policy Changes

  • Material changes will be notified via email at your registered address with at least 30 days advance notice.
  • Updated Policy published at growziq.in/privacy with a revised effective date.
  • Continued engagement after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
  • Changes do not apply retroactively to data already collected and processed.

13 Governing Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by Indian law including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023, IT Act 2000, IT (SPDI) Rules 2011, and Indian Contract Act 1872.

Disputes arising from this Policy are resolved by arbitration in Punjab, India as set out in the Terms of Service.
